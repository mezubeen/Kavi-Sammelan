Kavi Parichay

Chirag Jain

Chirag jain is one of the most popular hasya kavi of the country. His satires and comments captures headlines. His witty performances make audiences full of laughter. His poems convey a message to society along with a bunch of laughter. His Manch Sanchalan is very lively and full of energy.

Birth : 27 May 1985; New Delhi
Education : Master of Journalism & Mass Communication.

Published work
1) KOI YOOn HI NAHIn CHUBHTA (Poetry)
2) OS (Poetry)
3) MANN TO GOMUKH HAI (Poetry)
4) JAAGO PHIR EK BAAR (Research on the revolution of 1857)
5) PAHLI DASTAK (Edited -Poems of 15 budding poets)
6) DOOSRI DASTAK (Edited -Poems of 15 budding poets)
7) SHRADDHANJALI SHRAVAN RAHI KO (Tribute)
8) KAVI NIRDESHIKA (Directory)
9) Articles, poems and satires are regularly publishing with various news papers and magazines.

Other achievements
1) Broadcasted with various media i.e. Doordarshan, NDTV, Life OK, AAJ Tak, SAB TV, News Nation, News 18, News 24, ABP News, India TV, India News, Big Magic, Jain TV, Jansandesh, Dabang etc.
2) Recited poem more then 1000 kavi-sammelans all over the country.
3) Got popularity from laughter show titled LAUGH INDIA LAUGH on Life OK & CHUNAVI CHAKALLAS on NEWS NATION.
4) Visited overseas for kavi-sammelans i.e. Oman, Hongkong & Mucao.
